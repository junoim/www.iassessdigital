VAALO-TA




WHAT IS VAALO TA & HOW DOES IT WORK?

VAALO TA (Voice AI Assistant for LifelineRx Operations) is a mobile-based AI-powered healthcare assistant that reads and interprets prescriptions, reminds users to take medication, and provides voice-guided instructions. It especially caters to senior citizens, children, and those with limited education — helping bridge the gap between complex medical prescriptions and accessible, everyday healthcare.

Why It's Needed:

  • Understand handwritten prescriptions.
  • Keep track of dosages and timing.
  • Identify alternative medications or brand options.
  • Get reminders, especially in regional languages
VAALO TA turns treatment into action — with the help of AI, OCR, voice alarms, and reminders.

THE PROBLEM WE WANT TO ADDRESS

Key Healthcare Gaps:

  1. Patients often don't understand prescriptions, especially seniors and low-literacy users.
  2. No proper reminder system for dosage/frequency, especially across age groups.
  3. Lack of quick tools to identify alternative medicine compositions or brands.
  4. No existing system that acts as a Voice AI Assistant, mapping symptoms to medicine with human-like guidance.

WHAT ARE WE PROPOSING TO DESIGN AND IMPLEMENT?

  • Prescription Reader with AI/OCR:
    • Scans and extracts details from doctor's handwritten prescriptions using OCR and AI.
  • Voice Instructions Generator:
    • Converts medical information into understandable voice instructions tailored to age, condition, and language.
  • Daily Medication Reminders:
    • Voice alarms in English and local state languages to remind patients of upcoming dosages.
  • 24+ Hour Missed Alerts:
    • Alerts relatives or caregivers if critical medicine is not taken for over 24 hours.
  • Medical History Logs:
    • Stores scanned prescriptions and medication history for future reference.
  • Dosage/Frequency Voice Reminders:
    • Helps prevent missed doses with real-time, friendly voice alerts.

TARGET USERS

  • Senior Citizens - who need constant reminders and voice-based assistance.
  • Children - where parents or guardians manage dosage and scheduling.
  • Low-Literacy Groups - who may not read prescriptions easily.
  • Healthcare Providers - looking to simplify post-consultation care.
  • Family Members - who track critical medications for loved ones.

UNIQUE SELLING POINTS:

  1. Voice Alarm in any regional language
  2. Voice reminders: “You haven't taken your medicine yet!”
  3. AI-driven medical parsing
  4. Real-time alerts to relatives or caregivers
  5. Ability to ask doctors for composition mapping
  6. Offline and cloud-synced medication logs
