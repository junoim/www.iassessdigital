VAALO-TA

VAALO-TA is an innovative AI-powered medication assistant designed to support elderly individuals and those requiring regular medication management. Combining advanced technologies in speech recognition, computer vision, and smart monitoring, VAALO-TA ensures that users take the right medication at the right time safely and conveniently.The system automatically detects when a person picks up a medicine bottle, verifies the medication using image analysis, and guides the user with voice instructions. It also monitors dosage adherence and can send alerts or reminders as needed.With its intuitive and user-friendly design, VAALO-TA promotes independence, enhances medication safety, and reduces the risk of errors, making it an ideal companion for seniors and caregivers alike.
